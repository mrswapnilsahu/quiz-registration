TechnoStrife

Rules and regulations for the participants 1.   Online registration is the only way to participate in this event 2.   Maximum two students can participate in a team 3.   Each round will be time constraints based (time is fixed) 4.   No team can participate in next round after disqualifying. 5.   Mode of quiz is online, offline, audio & video rounds as well. 6.   The concept of buzzer round is FCFS. Team will play buzzer first  will get chance to answer first. 7.   Questions will be based on basic general science & general knowledge, which is common for all branches. 8.   In any round the final decision will be of our respected Judges. 9.   Any type of indiscipline will disqualify the team from event. 10.  Use of mobile phone, books or any other resource is not allowed for answering the questions. 11.  Participant must be on time for participation in each round 12. Teams will be selected for next round on the basis of merit list.
